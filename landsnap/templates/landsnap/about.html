About LandSnap

LandSnap is a powerful tool for analyzing land changes over time using comparative image analysis.

How It Works

  1. Upload two images of the same location taken at different times
  2. Our system processes the images and identifies changes
  3. View detailed analysis results with heatmap visualization

Technology

Using advanced computer vision algorithms, LandSnap detects even subtle changes in terrain, vegetation, and structures.
